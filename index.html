Hobbies

I've been passionate about biking for as long as I can remember. My journey started when I was a young kid, riding BMX bikes and quickly falling in love with the thrill of speed and the freedom that came with it. As I got older, my love for cycling evolved, and I transitioned into downhill racing, where I really started to excel. The adrenaline rush of racing down steep hills at high speeds was unmatched. My dedication and hard work paid off when I achieved some great results, which led to me being sponsored by a brand. It was an incredible experience being featured in the newspaper and even on TV, something I’ll never forget. As I grew up, I made the decision to retire from racing and shifted my focus to cross-country biking. These days, I still enjoy riding on weekends, but now I appreciate the more laid-back pace of cross-country riding. It’s still a great way to stay active and explore new places on two wheels. When I was 16, I also got into off-road motorcycling, which has been another major hobby of mine. I bought my first motorcycle and started riding off-road, and over the years, I've upgraded my bike for better performance. Riding through rugged terrain is another experience I absolutely love, and it's a fantastic way to get outside and challenge myself in a different way. Cycling and off-road riding have always been more than just hobbies to me, they’ve shaped who I am and have been a constant source of joy and adventure throughout my life.
